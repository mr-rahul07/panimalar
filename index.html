Welcome to Our College

Dr.jeppiaar(Founder)

Panimalar Engineering College is a premier institute established in 2000. The institute is located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu and it offers Degree courses including UG and PG programs. These programs are offered in Full-Time mode and are delivered by highly experienced faculty. Our college is dedicated to providing the best education and resources to our students. Explore our various modules to learn more about what we offer

Dr. P. CHINNADURAI (Secretary & Correspondent)

Candidates can pursue education in multiple courses like B.E. / B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech, MBA/PGDM in the stream of Engineering, Business & Management Studies. Panimalar Engineering College provides students with the benefit of the knowledge and skills acquired by its trained and experienced faculty in the fields of Civil Engineering, Communications Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics Engineering, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning.

Dr. C. Sakthi Kumar (Director)

The institute offers courses which the AICTE approves. Most importantly, these courses with 2616 seats are provided at affordable fees, which increases accessibility and allows students the opportunity to gain knowledge and skills in their chosen field. The courses offered by the institute are in the fee range of INR 35,000-200,000.

Dr.Saranya Sree Sakthi Kumar (Director)

Panimalar Engineering College also offers good infrastructure, such as Auditorium, Cafeteria, Hostel, Labs, Library, Shuttle Service, Sports Complex, etc. To be a premier institution recognized for fostering academic excellence, promoting research and innovation, and shaping future leaders who can drive technological advancements and sustainable development worldwide.

Dr. K.Mani (Principal)

Panimalar Engineering College is committed to providing high-quality technical education through innovative teaching methods, cutting-edge research, and a strong emphasis on ethical values. We aim to develop competent professionals who can contribute meaningfully to society and excel in a global environment.

We aim to foster a nurturing and supportive environment where students can excel academically and personally. Our mission is to equip students with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in their future endeavors.
